Privacy Policy for My Aquariums

My Aquariums respects your privacy.

My Aquariums is designed to help users explore freshwater species, organize aquariums, review compatibility guidance, and track aquarium maintenance.

Data Collection

My Aquariums does not collect personal data from the app. Any camera or photo access is used only for on-device functionality unless otherwise stated in a future update.

How Information Is Used

Aquarium information you create in the app, such as tank names, residents, and maintenance records, is stored only for app functionality on your device.

Data Sharing

My Aquariums does not sell personal information and does not share personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the app after updates means you accept the revised policy.
